Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BANE AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our AI Assistant via Facebook Messenger.

2. Information We Collect

When you message BANE AI, we receive basic profile information provided by Meta (Facebook), which may include your:

  • Name (First and Last)
  • Profile Picture
  • Language settings
  • Timezone
  • The content of messages and attachments you send to us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide AI-generated responses to your queries.
  • Maintain a temporary context of the conversation to improve response quality.
  • Isolate your improved user data (workspace/files) from other users.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is processed in real-time. Message history may be stored temporarily for conversation context but is isolated securely. We do not sell or share your personal message data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. User Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your conversation history at any time by sending the command /delete_history (if supported) or contacting the administrator.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the bot administrator directly via the platform.
